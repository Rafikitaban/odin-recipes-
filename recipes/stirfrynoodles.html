stirfrynoodles recipe

Preparation of stirfrynoodles

Ingredients

    cooked noodles,1/4 cup of cooking oil,vegetables,chopped onions and tomatoes,salt

    step 1:saute the chopped onions on the 1/4 cup of cooking oil in a frying pan step 2 : add the salt and after a few seconds of stirring, add the rew vegetables and saute them for 20 minutes until you see a soft texture step 3: add the tomatoes and saute again for five minutes while occasionaly stirring step 4: add the cooked noodles and stir the mixture till the mixture is uniform. step 5: serve while hot. best served with cold beverage.
