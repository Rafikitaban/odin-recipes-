spanish omelette recipe

Preparation of spanishomelette

Ingredients

    two large onions, three large tomatoes, 1/4 cup cooking oil, 6 eggs, salt and pepper

    step 1: crush the six eggs in a bowl and stir till they are all uniform step 2: chop the onions and tomatoes into equal pieces step 3: add the onions and tomatoes into the raw eggs and mix the ingredients step 4: add the salt and pepper to the mixture and stir one more time step 5: add the 1/4 cup of oil into a heating fry pan and after a few minutes, add the combined mixture into the pan step 6: cook for a few minutes then remove it when it is ready. Best served hot.
