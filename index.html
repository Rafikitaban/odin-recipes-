Odin Recipes

Description

Preparation of marinated chicken tenders and jolof rice.

Ingredients

    raw chicken tenders soy sauce 1 cup of rice seasonings(salt,paprika,ginger powder and pepper) 1/2 cup of cooking oil 2 cups of water

    step 1:boil the chicken tenders for 25 minutes and let it cool step 2:measure 1/4 cup of soysauce and add seasonings step 3:slowly mix the chicken tenders with the sauce and leave it for 30 minutes step 3:boil water and add 1/4 cup of cooking oil and salt step 4:add rice and let it simmer for 25 minutes and let it cool step 5:add the rest 1/4 cup of cooking oil in a pan and fry your seasoned chicken tenders step 6:serve when hot and enjoy your meal.
